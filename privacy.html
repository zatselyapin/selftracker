Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Self Tracker ("the App") is developed and maintained by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected.

1. Data Collection and Storage

The App stores all user-entered information (mood, energy level, productivity, thoughts, notes, and similar data) locally on your device only. The App does not transmit, upload, or share your data with any server or third party.

2. Optional Analytics (Future Versions)

Future versions of the App may include optional analytics (e.g., Firebase Analytics) to help improve user experience. If analytics are added, they will:

  • collect only anonymous usage data (such as screen usage or crash reports);
  • require explicit user consent when first enabled;
  • be fully described in an updated version of this Policy.

3. Health Data (Google Fit / Apple HealthKit — Future Feature)

The App may integrate with Google Fit or Apple HealthKit in future versions. Any access to health data will:

  • require explicit permission from the user;
  • never be uploaded, shared, or transmitted;
  • be used only for display or analysis within the App.

4. Advertising and Monetization

The initial version of the App contains no advertising or in-app purchases. Future monetization features (ads, subscriptions, or purchases) will be announced in advance and reflected in an updated version of this Privacy Policy.

5. Security

All data is stored on the user’s device. To protect your information, we recommend enabling system-level device security (PIN, password, or biometric lock).
